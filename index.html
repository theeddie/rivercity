About

Welcome to River City Merchandising

From concerts, entertainment acts, Monster Jams to Presidential Library Openings–we’re the Jack-of-all-trades in special events merchandising.

Who Are We?

Over four decades ago, River City Merchandising started as merchandise service providers that catered to a specific niche of acts and bands. With time, our customer base expanded from selected musicians to major venues. So far, we’ve handled every possible project–from small theatre performances to mega stadium-based shows. The company’s currently collaborating with several venues across America. We’re responsible for key operations in merchandising for various events organized under their roof.

Besides this, we’re often roped in at special events like Boxing championships, Monster Jam World Finals, and NCAA Final Fours. These experiences have helped us build strong ties within the music industry and event management circles. It makes it easier for us to bridge the gap between venues and the artists performing there.
